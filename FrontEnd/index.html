PM2.5 ↔ Disease Correlation

Positive Correlations — More Pollution = More Deaths

Note on negative correlations
Some chronic diseases show negative correlation because wealthier (higher-PM) urban areas have better healthcare access, reducing measured death rates despite higher disease burden.
